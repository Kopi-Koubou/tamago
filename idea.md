# Tamago - Duolingo for Cooking

## Elevator Pitch
Voice-first, gamified cooking app for 25-40yo home cooks who meal prep. Think Duolingo meets meal prep - interactive voice guidance, streaks, achievements, and social recipe sharing.

## Target User
- **Demographic:** 25-40 years old
- **Life stage:** Renting or own home, cooking for self/partner/kids
- **Behavior:** Meal preps weekly, occasionally cooks dinner at home
- **Pain point:** Lost on where to start, what to cook, how to store/reheat

## Core Insight
Meal prep culture is mainstream (freezing, SouperCubes, etc.) but people lack guided, gamified workflow. Voice-first UX solves "dirty hands" problem during cooking.

## Product Vision (Phased)

### Phase 1: Gamified Cooking Experience (MVP)
- Voice-powered AI guidance (Whisper STT + ElevenLabs TTS)
- Step-by-step cooking stages with gamification
- Storing + reheating guidance for meal prep
- Streaks, levels, achievements

### Phase 2: Socialized
- Share progress with friends
- Cooking challenges
- Leaderboards

### Phase 3: Recipe Marketplace
- User-generated recipes
- TikTok creator acquisition → "claim your recipe" loop
- Creator attribution + audience building

### Phase 4: Kitchen OS
- Voice inventory tracking ("I bought chicken, rice, carrots")
- AI meal planning ("what can I make with what I have?")
- Shopping list automation
- Full home kitchen management

## Monetization
Freemium (free recipes, paid meal plans + premium features)

## Success Metric
Trending on App Store (top 50 Food & Drink)

## Sprint Goal
3-5 day MVP sprint → iOS app with voice guidance + 10 curated recipes + gamification loop
